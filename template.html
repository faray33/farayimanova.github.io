Bootcamp 2020: Story of a Sphere

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2020). This Processing project demonstrates an understaing and implementation of multiple aspect taught during the bootcamp. The project represents a 3D figure - a sphere, that is in rotation about its axis. With every rotation, the sphere changes color and increases in size. When the sphere inevitably reaches a climax radius it bursts into confetti-like colorful particles that were making up the sphere itself
